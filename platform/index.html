FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What’s new for SoundMind?

SoundMind’s version 2, is our completely revamped app, including an all-new Artist Platform, Memory Games, and an addition of 10,000’s new soundscapes, binaural beats and more for our users to choose from. Our Artist Platform features singers and composers including American Idol and America’s Got Talent contestants, and more of your favorites.

We’re also launching SoundMind for Teams – tailored for your teams, schools, and organization to track the overall wellness journey of participants. You’ll be able to track metrics like moods, performance indicators, and even understand the happiness of your teammates. Wellness equates to job productivity, and we think you’re going to love this feature.

How do I know SoundMind works?

SoundMind has shown efficacy in all of its app testing, ranging from military personnel to nurses to regular civilians, like Gen-Z participants. In a recent study, the app helped decreased the heart rates of tested participants by 50%, and 64% of participants expressed an increase in feeling interested, active and excited after using the SoundMind app everyday for one week.

You can contact us to learn more along with sales and general inquiries at info@soundmind.app.

Who is the SoundMind app designed for?

Anyone. Literally anyone. Science has proven that music, sounds and ambience helps anyone decrease stress levels, improve productivity and increase happiness levels for a stronger, more healthier mental health.

What is the pricing for SoundMind for Teams?

Thanks for your interest in SoundMind for Teams. Contact our Partnerships Team to learn more about the pricing for this incredible tool to track the overall productivity & mental wellness benefits of your team or organization

Who started SoundMind?

SoundMind was founded by Gen-Z startup founders Brian Femminella and Travis Chen, in a pursuit to find the next mental health tool using sounds, music and beats. Founded in their college dorms, they are excited to have been able to bring SoundMind to the world with a world-class team.

Aside from our Artist Platform, our soundscapes and binaural beats are audio engineered by our in-house music development team — all made with love.
